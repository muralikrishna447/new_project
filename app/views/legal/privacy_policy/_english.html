Information We Collect Automatically

When you access or use our Services, we automatically collect information about you and associate it with information that you provide directly to us. For example, we may collect:

Transaction Information: When you make a purchase or a return, we collect information about the transaction, such as the class or product details, the transaction amount and the date of the purchase/return. For more information about how that information is collected, processed and stored, please refer to our payment processor’s privacy policy and terms.

Website Log Information: We log information about your use of our website, such as your computer’s operating system, the type of browser you use, access times, pages viewed, your IP address and the web page that referred you to our site.

Information Collected in Connection with Your Use of Joule: When you first pair your Joule to our mobile application, Joule will automatically link to your Breville account. For information about de-linking your Joule from your Breville account, please see “Your Choices” below. When you connect Joule to a Wi-Fi network, we collect information about the network (such as name and connection strength). We collect information about your use of Joule, including what and how you cook, as well as your cooking and recipe preferences. We also collect data from Joule’s sensors and components, such as total motor revolutions, atmospheric pressure, and bath temperature. Some technical and usage information is also saved on Joule. Please see “Use of Information” below to understand how we use this data.

Device Information: When you access or use our mobile sites or apps, we collect information about the mobile phone, tablet or other device you are using, including the hardware model, operating system and version, device identifiers, mobile network information and information about your use of our Services. When you connect the Joule to our mobile app via Wi-Fi, we also collect information about the devices that you use to control Joule.

Location Information: With your consent, we collect information about your location when you access or use one of our mobile applications, such as to enable the mobile application to communicate with your Joule. For more details about the collection of location information and the choices you have, please see “Your Choices” below.

Using pixel tags and other similar technologies: We also use pixel tags (also known as web beacons and clear GIFs) – tiny graphic images – to tell us what parts of our website you have visited or to measure the effectiveness of any searches you may do on our website. Pixel tags also enable us to sent your email in a format you can read and let us know when you have opened an email message from us. Some of our third-party vendors may also use cookies or other online tools including pixel tags on our website. Pixels and cookies may also collect and use the geographical location of your computer or mobile device. This location data is collected for the purpose of providing you with information regarding services which we believe may be of interest to you based on your geographic location, and to improve our location-based products and services.

We may contract with third-party advertising companies to serve ads on our behalf across the internet. To do this, they will collect information about your visits to our website and your interaction with our products and services. They may also use information about your visits to this and other websites to target advertisements. These targeted advertisements may appear on other sites that you visit. This information is collected using a cookie or pixel tag – industry standard technology used by most websites.

By aggregating information: We may combine personal information with information about your online and in-store transactions and other interactions with us, including your purchases. We may also combine your personal information with information we receive from other sources, such as joint marketing partners, public databases, publicly available pages on social media platforms and other third parties. We use this information for market research purposes and to improve the quality of the Services we offer. We reserve the right to disclose aggregated, anonymous data we have collected to third parties for any purpose.

Information Collected by Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies:

We and our service providers use various technologies, including cookies and web beacons, to collect information about you when you interact with our Services or emails, including information about your browsing and purchasing behavior. Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive or in device memory that help us improve our website and your experience, see which areas and features of our website are popular, and count visits. Web beacons are electronic images that we use on our website and in our emails that help us deliver cookies, count visits and understand usage and campaign effectiveness. This provision states that under a number of specific, expressly enumerated conditions, personal data can be transferred to the United States if it is necessary for the purposes of compelling legitimate interests pursued by the data exporter (the individual). Breville has performed a “legitimate interest” analysis. In particular, Breville has considered that individuals have an interest in knowing about new products and services as the ones they have ordered. We have also considered that you may send us photographs and social media contacts with the specific purpose to upload them so that they may be visible worldwide.
